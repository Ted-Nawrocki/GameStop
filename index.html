Ryan Cohen's $55.5 billion unsolicited proposal to acquire eBay is among the boldest M&A bids in recent memory — a company worth $11.9 billion pursuing a target nearly four times its size. If completed, Cohen envisions a combined marketplace capable of becoming a genuine rival to Amazon.

02 — Strategic Thesis

Cohen's
Ambition.

"eBay could be a legit competitor to Amazon."

Ryan Cohen, CEO — GameStop Corp. · The Wall Street Journal, May 3, 2026
01

Physical Meets Digital

In his letter to eBay Chairman Paul Kessler, Cohen described GameStop's approximately 1,600 U.S. retail locations as a national infrastructure layer for authentication, inventory intake, fulfillment, and live commerce. Paired with eBay's global online marketplace, the combination could give the entity both digital reach and physical presence — an asset mix Amazon has spent years attempting to replicate.

02

Category Alignment

The two businesses share significant overlap in video games, collectibles, and trading cards — categories eBay CEO Jamie Iannone has already elevated as strategic "focus categories." Cohen also identified live commerce as an underdeveloped opportunity for eBay's platform, a channel where GameStop's physical footprint and brand recognition among collectors could drive meaningful differentiation.

03

Operational Track Record

Since assuming control of GameStop in early 2021, Cohen oversaw a swing from a $381 million net loss in FY2021 to $418 million in net income in FY2025, while reducing SG&A costs by approximately $800 million. Cohen would serve as CEO of the combined company, compensated solely on the performance of the merged entity — no salary, no cash bonus, no golden parachute.

04 — Sequence of Events

From Accumulation
to Hostile Bid.

Drag or scroll to trace the deal's path from Cohen's compensation announcement through eBay's first public response.

January 2026
Cohen Compensation Structure Announced

GameStop's board grants Cohen 171.5 million performance-based stock options at a $20.66 exercise price — no salary, no cash bonus, no time-based vesting. The options could be worth more than $35 billion only if all nine tranches fully vest, which requires hitting both a $100 billion market cap and $10 billion in cumulative EBITDA. If the minimum hurdles are not met, Cohen receives nothing. Source: GameStop 8-K, SEC EDGAR, January 7, 2026.

February 4, 2026
GME Begins Accumulating EBAY

GameStop quietly begins building an economic position in eBay through direct share purchases and derivatives. eBay's closing price on this date becomes the "unaffected" baseline — the price GameStop uses to establish the 46% premium at announcement.

February 2026
eBay Announces Depop Acquisition

Separately, eBay agrees to acquire Depop from Etsy for approximately $1.2 billion in cash, targeting younger C2C fashion consumers. U.S. and German regulatory clearances obtained; UK and Australian reviews remain pending.

May 3, 2026 — Sunday
Hostile Proposal Submitted to eBay Board

Cohen submits a non-binding letter to eBay Chairman Paul Kessler offering $125.00 per share in a 50/50 cash-and-stock transaction. GameStop simultaneously discloses its 5% economic stake and provides the TD Securities "highly confident" financing letter. Cohen tells The Wall Street Journal he is prepared to pursue a proxy fight.

May 4, 2026 — Monday
eBay Confirms. Markets React.

eBay confirms receipt of the proposal, noting no prior discussions had taken place and its board would conduct a review. Cohen appears on CNBC Squawk Box in a notably combative interview. GME falls approximately 10%. EBAY rises approximately 5% to around $109 — a meaningful discount to the $125 offer.

Pending
Board Response Outstanding

The eBay board has confirmed receipt and initiated review. The proposal remains non-binding and unsolicited. If the board declines to engage, Cohen has stated his intent to pursue a proxy fight — taking the offer directly to eBay shareholders.

06 — Risk Factors

Material
Considerations.

Key factors that bear on deal feasibility and execution. This analysis is for educational purposes and does not constitute investment advice.

Financing Gap

Confirmed funding — $9.4 billion in balance-sheet cash plus the TD Securities $20 billion debt financing letter — totals approximately $29.4 billion. Completing the transaction at the proposed price requires additional equity issuance or third-party capital commitments that remain unconfirmed as of the announcement date.

Board Rejection and Proxy Path

eBay's board acknowledged receipt but confirmed that no prior discussions had taken place. If the board declines to engage substantively, Cohen has stated his willingness to pursue a proxy fight — a contested, expensive, and inherently uncertain process requiring GameStop to win sufficient eBay shareholder support against an incumbent board.

GME Shareholder Dilution

The 50% stock component of the consideration, combined with the likely need for additional equity issuance to fund the cash portion, implies significant dilution to existing GameStop shareholders. This dynamic drove GameStop's approximately 10% share price decline on the day of announcement.

Integration Complexity

Combining a domestic physical specialty retailer with a global online marketplace serving buyers and sellers in over 190 markets involves substantial operational, cultural, and systems integration risk. The pledged $2 billion in annual cost savings would need to be achieved concurrently with this integration within 12 months of close.

Regulatory Review

A transaction of this scale would require clearance from U.S. antitrust authorities and international regulators. eBay's separately announced acquisition of Depop from Etsy, with reviews still pending in the UK and Australia, further complicates the regulatory picture for a combined transaction.

No Prior Due Diligence

GameStop submitted this proposal without any prior engagement with eBay's management or access to non-public information. The $2 billion savings pledge and the overall strategic thesis are based entirely on publicly available data. Material unknown liabilities or undisclosed conditions could materially alter the transaction economics.
